Privacy Policy

How we handle information in connection with this website.

Last updated: 15 July 2026

Chalo Services LLC (“Chalo Services,” “we,” “us,” or “our”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect through the website at www.chaloservices.com (the “Website”), how we use it, and the choices you have.

This policy covers this website only. Our products — smtps.io, 5ymail.com, and iHosting.us — are operated as separate services, each with its own privacy policy. When you use one of those services, that service’s privacy policy applies to the information it collects.

1. A privacy-friendly website by design

This Website is a static, informational site. It does not require you to create an account, and it does not use advertising or third-party analytics or tracking technologies. We aim to collect as little information as possible.

2. Information you provide to us

The main way we receive information from you is when you choose to contact us:

  • Contact form. When you use the contact form on our Contact page, the form opens a message in your own email application, pre-filled with the details you entered (such as your name, email address, chosen topic, and message). The message is only sent to us if you send it from your email application. We receive that email at info@chaloservices.com.
  • Direct email. If you email us directly, we receive your email address, your name (if provided), and the contents of your message.

You do not need to provide any personal information simply to browse the Website.

3. Information collected automatically

The Website is hosted by a third-party hosting provider (see below). Like most web hosts, our provider may automatically log limited technical information — such as your IP address, browser type, and the pages requested — for the purpose of serving the site securely and reliably. We do not use this information to identify you and do not combine it with other data to build a profile of you.

4. Cookies

This Website does not set its own cookies and does not use cookies for advertising, analytics, or tracking. Your browser may still cache static files (such as images or fonts) as part of normal operation.

5. Third-party services we rely on

To deliver the Website, we use a small number of third-party service providers who may process limited technical data on our behalf or as independent controllers:

  • Website hosting. The Website is hosted on GitHub Pages (provided by GitHub, Inc., a Microsoft company). Their servers process requests to the Website and may log technical information as described above.
  • Web fonts. The Website loads fonts from Google Fonts. When your browser requests these fonts, Google may receive your IP address and technical request information. We do not use Google Fonts for advertising or tracking.

These providers process information under their own privacy policies. We encourage you to review them if you would like more detail.

6. How we use information

We use the information you provide to:

  • Respond to your enquiries and communicate with you;
  • Provide information about our products and services that you have requested;
  • Operate, maintain, and secure the Website;
  • Comply with legal obligations and protect our legal rights.

7. How we share information

We do not sell or rent your personal information. We only share it in these limited circumstances:

  • With service providers who help us operate the Website or respond to you (such as our email and hosting providers), acting on our behalf;
  • Where required by law, regulation, legal process, or governmental request;
  • To protect the rights, property, or safety of Chalo Services, our users, or others.

8. Data retention

We keep enquiry emails and related correspondence only for as long as needed to respond to you and for our legitimate business and legal purposes, after which we delete or archive them. You may ask us to delete your correspondence at any time (see below).

9. Your rights and choices

Depending on where you live, you may have rights over your personal information — such as the right to access, correct, or delete it, or to object to or restrict certain processing. Because we collect so little information, the simplest way to exercise these rights is to contact us at info@chaloservices.com. We will respond as required by applicable law. We will not discriminate against you for exercising any of these rights.

10. Children’s privacy

The Website is intended for a general, business audience and is not directed to children under the age of 13 (or the equivalent minimum age in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it.

11. International visitors

Chalo Services is based in the United States, and the Website is hosted and operated there. If you access the Website from outside the United States, any information you provide will be transferred to and processed in the United States, which may have data-protection laws different from those in your country.

12. Your responsibility

You are responsible for the information you choose to share with us and for your use of the Website and of any products or services offered or linked through it, which you use at your own risk. To the fullest extent permitted by law, Chalo Services LLC and its owner(s) accept no responsibility or liability for how you or any other person uses the Website or the services, or for any outcome resulting from that use. The handling of your information under this policy does not create any additional liability on our part beyond what is required by applicable law. Please also review our Terms & Conditions.

13. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the “Last updated” date above. Material changes will be reflected on this page, and your continued use of the Website after changes take effect constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

14. Contact us

If you have any questions or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or your information, please contact us at info@chaloservices.com or through our contact page.
